What do students see on the Description page?

Initially a student sees much the same as a teacher sees on the Description page, including the description and the criteria. Students don't have tabs at the top of the page and cannot edit criteria. Students do have a guide to what stage they are up to in the reviewing process, and a submission facility at the bottom of this page.

After student's submit, they are taken through the stages of reviewing.

When a student has completed reviews, they are shown an overview page which displays:

  • details of their submission and marks;
  • information about the quality of reviews they have completed; and
  • feedback from their peers and teachers (review criteria and comments).

At all stages, students can see the description. It is sometimes compacted for space, but it is always available to them.
